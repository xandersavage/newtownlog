About Company

We Always Think On Your Dream

You can own your home without sweating the details

Newtownlogs is a Logging company that have been active for years, and we are proud of what we have accomplished within these period of time.

TAILORED TO YOUR NEEDS

North American forests grow large volumes of high-value species with physical properties and decorative characteristics that are appreciated globally. Newtown Logs offers hardwood logs and scanned logs from the Northeast and Midwest regions of North America from a large variety of species. Our hardwood expertise, consistent quality, supply reliability and unsurpassed levels of service allow log customers to focus on their core businesses and worry less about procurement challenges.

In addition to logs, Newtown Logs expertise in hardwood sourcing is available to its customer base: We also offer standing timber, timberland and forest management services.

WHAT WE OFFER

Newtown Logs provides hardwood logs from a variety of North American species:
  • Walnut
  • White Oak
  • Black Cherry
  • Hard Maple
  • Red Oak
  • White Ash
  • Hickory
  • Poplar
  • And many others

In each species, different grades are available: Slicer logs, Rotary logs, Saw logs.

Once newtown Logs understands customer needs from a species and grade standpoint, we define a specialized product program to meet requirements. Most products are available as controlled wood or FSC® 100%. Standing timber & timberland

Newtown Logs owns timberland and manages forests with long-term value creation in mind. While harvesting decisions are driven by silvicultural considerations, there are times when we offer standing timber to customers. As an owner of timberland, Newtown Logs periodically divests parcels in Indiana, New York and Pennsylvania.

WHY BUY FROM NEWTOWNLOGS?

Expertise: We employ more than 30 foresters and log and timber buyers throughout North America. Combined, these professionals have 400-plus years of industry experience. The expertise of the field team as well as the log yard staff allows for exceptional guidance and unsurpassed consistency.

Reliability: Newtown Logs has the largest network of buyers for high-end hardwood logs in the Northeast and Midwest regions of North America, so we’re able to reliably source a broad range of customer-requested logs. Our dedicated sales force excels at providing customers with a reliable log buying experience.

Support: Our skilled procurement team is committed to fulfilling every order in an expedient and focused way. Our sales and shipping provide a high level of service that extends beyond the point of sale.

HOW DO YOU BENEFIT?

Lower cost: Customers who buy Newtown Logs from North America enjoy several benefits compared to alternative sources of the material. Specifically, they will:

  • Save procurement time and dollars due to supply reliability.
  • Reduce waste, avoid mismatch of raw material and improve yield due to supply consistency.
  • Additional cost savings and yield gains are realized when customers opt to have logs scanned for more information about raw material, for better utilization.

    • More time for your core business Manufacturers are able to focus on their business without worrying about the log procurement process: They leave issues such as soil diversity, timber qualities and log characteristics to us, freeing them to spend more time on strategic imperatives.
