CT-scanned logs

ONE CLICK TO GUARANTEED QUALITY

Old town Logs is the first company to offer hardwood logs for sale with scanned internal images of individual logs. This way customers can now look inside a log before manufacturing or even purchasing. The combination of external photos and internal images offers a unique perspective on individual logs, eliminates the need to travel, and preserves the familiarity of traditional buying methods. This innovative process ensures you buy only exactly what you need, saving time and money.

WHAT WE OFFER

Old town Logs provides transparent quality hardwood logs in major North American species such as Cherry, Red Oak, White Oak, Hard Maple, Walnut and others.

Each log comes with detailed information on length, diameter, BFT, price, origin, parcel location, etc.

An actual photo of each log along with a scanned internal image is provided.

We assist in the global shipping process, supplying all export documents needed.

WHY BUY FROM OLDTOWN LOGS?

More information - better decision making
Oldtown Logs is one of the hardwood company that offers CT-scanned logs to assist log buyers with procurement of raw material. We make the critical task of buying hardwood logs from North America significantly easier, lowering procurement risk by offering new levels of transparency derived from technology and innovation.
